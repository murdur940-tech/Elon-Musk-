Timeline of Achievements 🚀

  • 1995 — Zip2 Founded
    Elon Musk co-founded Zip2, a software company providing city guides for newspapers.
  • 1999 — X.com Founded
    An online banking company that later became PayPal.
  • 2002 — SpaceX Founded
    Aerospace company aiming to make life multi-planetary.
  • 2004 — Tesla Joined
    Invested in Tesla Motors and became chairman, later CEO.
  • 2015 — OpenAI Co-Founded
    Organization focused on artificial intelligence research.
  • 2016 — Neuralink Founded
    Developing brain-computer interfaces.
  • 2018 — Falcon Heavy Launch
    SpaceX launched the most powerful rocket in operation.
  • 2020 — Crew Dragon Mission
    First private company to send astronauts to ISS.
  • 2022 — Twitter Acquired (X)
    Purchased Twitter and rebranded it as X.
  • Future — Mars Colonization
    Mission to establish human settlement on Mars
